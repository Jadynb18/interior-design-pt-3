Color theory is the science behind how colors work together so that way you can express whatevery type of vibe you wante people to feel when they enter a room. For example when you walk into a room with cool tone colors like sage green, blues or purple you feel a sense of calm and relaxation. On the other hand when you walk into a room of warm colors like red, orange or yellow you tend to feel a sense of energy a mood shift if you will. Another thing that interior designers look at in color theory is the different tones of the colors and depending on the color can really change the whole vibe of the room.
